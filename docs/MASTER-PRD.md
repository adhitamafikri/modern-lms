# Master PRD
This is the Master PRD that covers all the features that should be created in this platform

## What you have to know
This is a modern LMS platform with 2-party system:
- **Admin**
  - As the owner of this product who supplies and manages the courses in the platform
  - You can manage type of subscription that will be available in the system
  - Manage enrollments, onboardings, categories, courses, modules, and lessons
- **Student**:
  - Registers to our platform 
  - Subscribe to our platform
  - Enroll to the courses watching the lesson video, learning on the lesson content, .etc
